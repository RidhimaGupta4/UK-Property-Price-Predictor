Feature Engineering — Key Interactions
Derived features that improve model accuracy beyond raw inputs
region_price_index
Target encodes region using median price. Single most powerful predictor — explains 41.9% of price variance.
location_score
Composite: region index − commute − distance − crime − flood. Single location quality signal. Explains 16.3%.
bed_area
Bedrooms × Floor area (m²). Captures that size AND rooms together matter more than either alone. Explains 15.1%.
amenity_score
Garden×3 + Parking×2 + Garage×2 + School score + EPC score. Single composite amenity index rather than 5 separate features.
commute_x_urban
Commute minutes × urban density flag. Penalises long commutes more heavily in cities where alternatives exist.
epc_x_age
EPC score × age band score. Newer AND energy-efficient homes get a compounded green premium beyond either factor alone.
